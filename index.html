Total Annual Energy Consumption for New York City

Data Source: Modi Research Group
Bytes of the Big Apple

The map represents the annual building energy consumption at the block by block level and at the lot level( available when you zoom in further) for New York City, expressed in kilowatt hours per square meter. The data comes from a mathematical model based on statistics, not private information from utilities, to estimate the annual energy consumption values of buildings throughout the five boroughs and were able to break down what type of energy is being used, for which purpose and in what quantity.

Map created by the Modi Research Group with data from Bytes of the Big Apple
